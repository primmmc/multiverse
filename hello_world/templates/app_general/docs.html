HOW TO USE MULTIVERSE

Multiverse is designed for businesses and individuals who want to perform text sentiment analysis for customer-related data. It can be beneficial for customer service teams, marketers, product developers, and anyone interested in understanding the sentiment and feedback of their customers.

To input data into Multiverse, you typically need to follow these steps:

- Start the app by clicking on button in "home" page.
- Access the data input section, which be in the form of file upload(.CSV), or API integration.
- Enter or upload the text data you want to analyze.
- Select the button .
- Start the analysis process, and Multiverse will provide you with the sentiment insights based on the input data.

The safety of personal information is crucial. It's important to check the app's privacy and security policies to ensure that your personal data is protected. Multiverse, like other applications, should have measures in place to safeguard user data. Look for features such as data encryption, secure authentication, and compliance with data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA, etc.). Review the app's privacy policy and terms of use to understand how they handle your data.

To get in touch with the Multiverse app's support or customer service team, you can typically find contact information on their website or within the app itself. Look for a "Contact Us" section, which might include options such as email, a contact form, or a support hotline. You can reach out to them with any questions, issues, or feedback you may have about using the app.
